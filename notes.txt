these are notes for the basic layout of the website.

front page:
    login form, registration form, langtank description,
    website translations
profile page:
    username, user picture, languages speaking,
    languages learning, friends, country, last login,
    url has user's id, generate people who are online for 
    your learning languages
other profiles:
    profile page, message user, is user online?,
    chat with user
search page:
    search for friends by language, by country, by name,
    display most recently logged on
chat app:
    has shortcuts for highlighting and putting old text 
    in your new message, short for adding highlighted sentence 
    to SRS, automatic translation option
settings page:
    srs configuration(format of your SRS cards: L1 to L2,
    L2 to L1, both, etc)
